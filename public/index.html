How to play Tong-its

Three players (or 1v1), one 52-card deck. Empty your hand by forming melds, or hold the fewest points when the round is called. The dealer holds 13 and starts; turns go clockwise; the winner deals next.

Your turn

  • Draw from the draw pile — or from the discard only if its top card completes a meld for you (it glows gold when it does).
  • Arrange & meld — drag cards to reorder, drag them together in the tray to build groups. A green group is a legal meld: lay it, or keep it in hand.
  • Discard one card (drag it onto the discard pile, or arm + Discard) to end your turn.

Melds & winning

  • Set: 3–4 same rank, different suits. Run: 3+ in sequence, same suit (Ace low).
  • Sapaw: tap a card to arm it, then tap a glowing table meld to add it.
  • Tongits: empty your hand — instant win. Call before drawing (after a quiet cycle) for lowest count. Never melded = burned (can't call or win).

Turn timer: you have 20s per turn; if it runs out your lowest card is auto-discarded. AI takes 8s.
